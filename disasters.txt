Severe flood hits Mumbai causing evacuations
Heavy rainfall over the past 48 hours has submerged several areas of Mumbai.
Rescue teams have been deployed and NDRF units are on standby.
Thousands of residents have been moved to relief camps.

Earthquake tremors felt in Delhi
A mild earthquake of magnitude 4.2 was felt in Delhi early morning.
No casualties have been reported so far.
Authorities are monitoring the situation.

Severe flooding disrupts life in Kolkata after continuous rainfall
Kolkata witnessed severe flooding after 72 hours of continuous heavy rainfall caused major waterlogging across several parts of the city including Salt Lake, Behala, and Park Circus. Roads were submerged, public transport was disrupted, and many low-lying areas were cut off. Emergency response teams were deployed to rescue stranded residents, and relief camps were set up by local authorities. No official death toll has been reported so far, but several people were injured due to collapsing structures and electric shock incidents.

Cyclone approaches Chennai coast triggering evacuation alerts
A powerful cyclone is moving towards the Chennai coastline with wind speeds exceeding 110 km/h.
Coastal areas have been placed under high alert and evacuation orders have been issued.
Fishing activities have been suspended and disaster management teams are on standby.
Residents in low-lying regions are being shifted to cyclone shelters.
The severity of the cyclone is being closely monitored by meteorological authorities.
